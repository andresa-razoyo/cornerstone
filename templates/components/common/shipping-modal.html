Shipping Times & Tracking

  • Standard delivery (ground): Your order will arrive within 10 business days
  • Rush Processing: Move your order to the front of the line when you select rush processing during checkout. Rush orders placed by 9am CT will be processed and shipped the same day (excluding weekends and company-designated holidays). Orders received after 9am CT will ship the next business day. Standard shipping timelines still apply. Consult the UPS website for transit time estimates.
  • 2nd day air: Orders placed by 12:00 pm CT on a business day will arrive 2 business days after the order was placed. Orders placed after 12:00 pm on a business day will arrive 3 business days after the order was placed.
  • APO/FPO Priority mail:
    • Priority mail - Packages are typically delivered to the final destination within 14 business days after the order is placed.
    • Standard delivery (ground) - Packages are delivered to the APO/FPO facility in the U.S. within 10 business days. Packages are then shipped by the military to foreign bases when space is available. This may take 6-8 weeks for final delivery to the recipient.
